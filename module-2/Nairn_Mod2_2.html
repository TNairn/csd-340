Chapter 1: All About Me

I was born in West Palm Beach, Florida, and lived in Lake Worth until I was 7 years old. Then, I moved to Cape Coral, Florida where I lived for the rest of my adolescent years. After high school I moved to the Denver area of Colorado, but I still consider Cape Coral to be my hometown.

My childhood was pretty typical. I spent a lot of time playing with my friends in the neighborhood, and I stayed out of trouble for the most part. My parents encouraged me to get good grades in school, and I did okay at that.

My hobbies have had some variation throughout the years, but music has always been a part of my life. I was in band all through grade school, and I have played guitar since I was 4. In middle school I picked up electronic music production and I still enjoy both guitar and producing as a hobby. Video games have also been a hobby of mine, and since moving to Colorado I have developed a love for downhill skiing.

I have a few family members that live in different parts of the United States, but most of my extended family lives in Florida. Most of the family members I have a relationship with come from my dad's side and spending time with them around the holiday season was always important. Since moving to Colorado 6 years ago, it has been more difficult to see them, but I still make the time to visit.
